# Paris R. Sharpe

Charlotte, NC | Founder, strategist, and applied AI builder

I build systems that flip extractive dynamics in regulated industries. Aviation infrastructure that returns airspace value to property owners. Patent tools that let inventors file their own work without an attorney gatekeeper. Local business discovery that ranks by tenure and independence rather than review volume. AI governance that makes agentic adoption legible to the people who have to defend it.

Every project answers three questions before I ship: what is the dominant system extracting from people here, what legal or regulatory foundation makes an inversion defensible, and what business model makes the inversion sustainable at scale.

### Independence-first developer tooling

The three MCPs below share a thesis. The same algorithmic-intermediary dynamic that disadvantages small operators in commerce is the same one that disadvantages property owners in airspace, inventors in patent prosecution, and ordinary people in nearly every system where intermediary access decides who gets seen.

**[local-roots-mcp](https://github.com/parissharpe/local-roots-mcp).** Independence-first local business discovery MCP. Rewards long tenure, low marketing footprint, family ownership, and single-location operation. Filters national chains. Built on the Google Places API with an opinionated scoring engine.

**[pro-se-uspto-mcp](https://github.com/parissharpe/pro-se-uspto-mcp).** USPTO procedural tools for pro se inventors filing their own patents. Entity status, fee calculation, deadline tracking, IDS rules, form lookup. Pure rule-based logic, no API key required.

**[handoff-mcp](https://github.com/parissharpe/handoff-mcp).** Local-first MCP server bridging Claude Cowork and Claude Code with shared working memory. Your strategic context stays on your machine.
